Noah Sylvester Peterson

Astronomer • Computer Scientist • Mathematician • Physicist

Bio

Noah Sylvester Peterson is a hard working and self motivated student and researcher with a wide reservoir of knowledge.
In his youth Noah enjoyed playing sports like soccer, hockey, base ball, fencing, and archery. He was also captain of his FTC robotics team and directed community plays.
In his undergraduate career Noah received four majors (Astronomy, Computer Science, Mathematics, and Physics) and one minor (biology). Due to his extensive list of degrees he was active in many interdisciplinary projects and led the MISSFIT interdisciplinary research collaboration. He was also an avid volunteer at the Drake Municipal Observatory.
Noah is currently attending the University at Boulder Colorado and earning a PhD in computer science. He works with the SWORD group assimilating space weather observations into the JEDI data assimilation framework.
